DJI Mavic Air 2: This is one of the most advanced and feature-packed drones available in the market today. It features a 48MP camera, can shoot 8K videos, and has a flight time of up to 34 minutes.
DJI Mavic 2 Pro: This drone is similar to the Mavic Air 2 but has a more professional-grade camera and supports better battery life. It features a 20MP camera, can shoot 4K videos at 60fps, and has a flight time of up to 31 minutes.
DJI Spark: This is a smaller and more portable drone that is perfect for beginners and hobbyists. It features a 12MP camera, can shoot 4K videos, and has a flight time of up to 16 minutes.
